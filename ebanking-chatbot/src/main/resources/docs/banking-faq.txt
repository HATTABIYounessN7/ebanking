DIGITAL BANKING - KNOWLEDGE BASE

=== ACCOUNT TYPES ===

Current Account (CA):
- Designed for everyday transactions
- Comes with an overdraft facility of up to 9000 MAD
- Ideal for salary deposits and regular payments
- No minimum balance required

Saving Account (SA):
- Designed for long-term savings
- Earns interest at 5.5% per year
- Best for growing your savings over time
- Minimum opening balance recommended: 1000 MAD

=== ACCOUNT STATUSES ===

CREATED: Account has just been opened and is being processed.
ACTIVATED: Account is fully active and operational.
SUSPENDED: Account has been temporarily suspended due to 
           suspicious activity or customer request.

=== OPERATIONS ===

Credit (Deposit):
- Add money to an account
- No upper limit on deposits
- Reflected immediately in account balance

Debit (Withdrawal):
- Remove money from an account
- Cannot exceed current balance
- Operation is rejected if balance is insufficient

Transfer:
- Move money between two accounts
- Requires valid source and destination account IDs
- Source account must have sufficient balance
- Both accounts must be active

=== SECURITY ===

- All sessions are secured with JWT (JSON Web Token)
- Tokens expire after 24 hours
- All API calls are authenticated
- Sensitive data is encrypted at rest and in transit

=== COMMON ISSUES ===

Insufficient balance error:
This means your account does not have enough funds for the 
requested debit or transfer. Either deposit funds first 
or reduce the amount.

Account suspended:
Contact support immediately at support@digitalbank.ma 
or call our helpline to reactivate your account.

Forgotten account ID:
Your account ID is visible in the web application under 
the Accounts section. You can also ask the chatbot to 
list your accounts if you are authenticated.

Transaction not showing:
Transactions are reflected immediately. If a transaction 
is missing, please contact support with the date and amount.
